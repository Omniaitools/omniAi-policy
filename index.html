Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

At omniAi, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data, especially with our AI integration features and community tools.

1. AI Data Processing

Our application features AI tools powered by third-party APIs (e.g., Groq, Llama).

  • Conversations: Chat data processed by our AI is sent securely to API providers for processing only. We do not store your chat logs on our servers.
  • Local Storage: Your generated prompts and tool history are saved locally on your device's memory to enhance your experience. You can clear this history at any time.

2. Camera & Image Data (Prompt Extractor)

Our "Prompt Extractor" tool requires access to your device camera or gallery to function.

  • Image Processing: Images you upload are processed through high-speed AI Vision models to generate text prompts.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not save, store, or share your personal photos. Images are discarded immediately after the analysis is complete.

3. User-Generated Content (Ai Community)

Our application includes a public chat feature ("Ai Community"). We have a strict zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content, harassment, or abusive users.

  • Reporting & Blocking: Users can report inappropriate messages and block abusive users directly within the chat interface.
  • Moderation: We actively moderate this community. We reserve the right to instantly remove content and permanently ban users who violate our community guidelines without prior notice.

4. Local-First Security & API Keys

To ensure maximum security, many of our tools run entirely on your device:

  • User API Keys: For advanced features (e.g., Video Prompt Architect), you may enter your own third-party API keys. These keys are stored strictly locally on your device. We do not collect, intercept, or store your personal API keys on our servers.
  • Code Lab: Executes code within your device's secure browser sandbox.
  • UI Settings: Your theme preferences and saved notes are stored locally on your device only.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted providers to power our services:

  • Groq Cloud: Powers our real-time AI responses.
  • Google AdMob: May collect non-identifiable technical data (device ID/IP) to serve relevant advertisements.
  • External AI Links: Our directory links to third-party sites. Each has its own privacy policy which we encourage you to read.

6. Permissions Required

  • Camera: Used only for analyzing images via Prompt Extractor.
  • Storage: To allow you to pick images from your gallery.
  • Internet: To connect with AI models and fetch tool data.

7. Compliance

We comply with standard privacy regulations by minimizing data collection. We do not require account registration (Email, Phone, etc.) for our core tools, ensuring you remain anonymous while using the app.
