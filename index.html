Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

At omniAi, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data, especially with our new AI integration features.

1. AI Data Processing (OmniAI Agent)

Our application features an AI assistant powered by third-party APIs (Groq/Llama).

  • Conversations: Chat data with the OmniAI Agent is sent to Groq APIs for processing only. We do not store your chat logs on our own servers.
  • Local Storage: Your chat history is saved locally on your device's memory to enhance your experience. You can clear this history at any time using the "Delete" icon.

2. Camera & Image Data (Prompt Extractor)

Our "Prompt Extractor" tool requires access to your device camera or gallery to function.

  • Image Processing: Images you upload are processed through high-speed AI Vision models to generate prompts.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not save, store, or share your personal photos. Images are discarded immediately after the analysis is complete.

3. Local-First Tools

To ensure maximum security, many of our tools run entirely on your device:

  • Code Lab: Executes code within your device's secure browser sandbox.
  • UI Settings: Your theme preferences, font sizes, and "Pinned Tools" are stored locally on your device only.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted providers to power our services:

  • Groq Cloud: Powers our real-time AI responses.
  • Google AdMob: May collect non-identifiable technical data (device ID/IP) to serve relevant advertisements.
  • External AI Links: Our directory links to third-party sites. Each has its own privacy policy which we encourage you to read.

5. Permissions Required

  • Camera: Used only for analyzing images via Prompt Extractor.
  • Storage: To allow you to pick images from your gallery.
  • Internet: To connect with AI models and fetch tool icons.

6. Compliance

We comply with GDPR and CCPA standards by minimizing data collection. We do not require account registration, ensuring you remain anonymous while using our tools.
