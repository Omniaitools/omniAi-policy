Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

At omniAi, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your personal data remains secure.

1. Local-First Data Processing

To ensure maximum security and privacy, omniAi is designed as a local-first utility suite. This means:

  • Code Lab: All source code you write is executed locally within your device's secure sandbox. We do not store or transmit your code to any external servers.
  • Prompt Architect & Image Studio: Prompt generation is handled using local logic and templates. Your creative inputs remain on your device.
  • Color Lab & Content Planner: Data is processed locally to provide instant results without the need for cloud storage.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number. We do not require account registration to access our core productivity tools.

3. Third-Party Services

To support the continued development of omniAi, we may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements. These partners may collect non-identifiable technical data (such as device ID or IP address) to serve relevant ads.
  • External AI Links: Our directory provides links to third-party AI websites. Please note that these sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices.

4. Permissions Required

The application may request certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Internet Access: Necessary for loading external tools, favicons, and displaying advertisements.
  • Clipboard Access: Allows you to copy generated prompts, code, and color hex codes for use in other applications.

5. Compliance with Laws

We are committed to complying with international data protection standards, including GDPR and CCPA, where applicable, by minimizing data collection and empowering user control.
